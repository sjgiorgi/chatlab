Advanced Customization
======================

Enhance your bots with personality, appearance, and content safeguards.  
These features define how your bot *feels* and ensure ethical, controlled
interactions with participants.

Typical setup includes defining a persona to guide tone and style, assigning
an avatar for visual identity, and setting moderation thresholds to prevent
unsafe or inappropriate content.

Topics Covered
--------------

- **Personas:** Define tone and personality  
- **Avatars:** Add visual identity to bots or participants  
- **Moderation:** Configure content filters and thresholds
